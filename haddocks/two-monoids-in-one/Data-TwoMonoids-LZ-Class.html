mprodZ :: ZList () a -> a Source #

mprodZ is a ZList () algebra.

mprodZ . fmap mprodZ === mprodZ . join

When you override the default implementation, it must keep the relation to the other methods:

mprodZ Nil === one
mprodZ (Zee _) === zero
mprodZ (pure a) = mprodZ (Cons a Nil) === a
