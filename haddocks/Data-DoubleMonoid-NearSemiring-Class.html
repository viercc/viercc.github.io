class DMLZ a => NearSemiring a Source #

NearSemiring is law-only subclass of DoubleMonoid with additional left zero law (hence DMLZ) and left distribution law.

-- Left distribution
-- (it is called "right distributivity" more commonly)
(x /+/ y) /*/ z === (x /*/ z) /+/ (y /*/ z)
