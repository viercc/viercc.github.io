msumZ :: ZList a -> a Source #

(zero, /+/) is a monoid with a right zero element one. This can be stated as msumZ is a ZList algebra.

msumZ . fmap msumZ === msumZ . join

When you override the default implementation, it must keep the relation to the other methods:

msumZ Nend === zero
msumZ Zend === one
mprodZ (Cons a Nend) === a
